What is the Norwich Testing Initiative?

The Norwich Testing Initiative (NTI) is a newly formed initiative which will launch a large-scale screening pilot for SARS-CoV-2, the virus which causes the COVID-19 disease. The pilot begins on Monday 7 January 2021 and runs for four weeks.

How do I participate?

To participate we ask that you register using our Sign-up page and book one testing slot per week for two consecutive weeks.

Testing Slot Informations

We offer two testing slots for each working day, one in the morning and one in the afternoon to best accomodate our participants

What we will require from you?

In order to help with track and tracing, participants will need to provide some personal infomration when they sign up. Information such as forename, surname, age gender and ethnicy are all required. Additonally, we will need your contact details such as your phone number, date of birth, house number, postcode and email address to inform and advise those who test positive.
